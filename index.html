Flat-rate pricing

No guessing. No surprise hourly bill.

We do not charge by the hour. Moon & Moss uses flat-rate pricing based on the size of the space, condition of the home, number of rooms, and the type of cleaning needed.

This means you’ll know your price before we arrive — and we’ll know exactly what your clean includes.

For the best quote, send:

  • Number of bedrooms and bathrooms
  • Rooms you want cleaned
  • Type of clean needed
  • Photos of the space
  • Any special requests or problem areas
